## Overview
### Who We Are
  1. Emerging technologies think tank.
  2. Experts in AI strategy, governance, and cybersecurity.
  3. Registered 501(c)(3) non-profit and international NGO.

#### What We Do
  1. Focus on how changing technologies impact societies and the way people work.
  2. Develop global strategies for AI, cybersecurity, and effective technology governance.
  3. Export knowledge and strategic capabilities, not cultural values.
  4. Create economic independence and digital sovereignty that support regional goals and respect cultural differences.

#### How Our NGO Programs are Different
  1. Vendor-neutral education, certification, and attestation programs that create global competitive advantage.
  2. Partnering with local organizations and individuals to ensure educational quality and relevance based on regional goals.
  3. Issue internationally-recognized credentials that enhance trust in the region.

## Mission Objectives
The growing intersection of data, privacy, and technology requires new ways of thinking about information security and
how it impacts both business and society. Our is to provide cutting-edge thought leadership, education, and training
that reframes traditional security leadership for the 21st century by addressing traditional cybersecurity, risk
management, and other related topics within a global social context.

## Actionable Solutions
The institute provides practical, consensus-based recommendations and practical solutions. Each consensus is reached
through discussion and debate among the panel's cross-functional members.
